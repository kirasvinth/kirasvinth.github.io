Welcome!

Our food is fresh and prepared to order. We use as many local ingredients as possible. Our aim is to keep our prices affordable, our atmosphere relaxed and casual and our menu flexible to our customers’ choices.

We have a unique Kid’s Menu with just the right portions and pricing for those finicky young ones. We also offer espresso drinks, soft drinks and beer & wine. Our desserts are tasty enough to bring you back for more, but we think the entire menu will have you eating Zazzle-style often!

Come on in to Café Zazzle and find out why the readers of the Petaluma Magazine and the North Bay Bohemian voted us Best Restaurant in Sonoma county, & Jeff Cox of the Press Democrat gave us 3-star rating!
